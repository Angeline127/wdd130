Welcome to Charlie Girls Coding!

Charlie is proud to have you join the club, we are here to help and encourage each other. We are a nonprofit club dedicated to delivering quality service to all women around the world. All of our proceeds contribute to building a state-of-the-art in technology in the world.

Purpose

I am a girl who loves to code, I have built websites for top companies in the world. When you need a place where they teach girls coding, where do you? Do you know that girls can code very well when they are being taught very well? That is what my Business does.

We teach girls coding, we explain the importance of why a girl should code, regardless of her age, knowledge about coding, and the current field she is pursuing. Are you a girl who wants to learn how to code effectively? Then we got you covered! Are you a girl who is optimistic about learning new things? Do you want to be your own boss and build websites for companies? Then you are at the right place my girl. Our mission is to help girls code and build websites

About Programs

Online Study Programs

There will be classes each week for all. We will begin by getting to know the basics, then we move on to the intermediate and the we will move on to a pro yeah! all will be how consistent you are; we will learn programs like python, javascript, java, sql etc.. We will also learn web developemnt that will be html, css and javascript. There will also be UI UX design learn. Girl it will be an awesome journey.

Weekly Quiz

The particpants will be quiz every week based on what they learnt in the week. It will be fun because there will be a reward for the person who will make it to the top three after all the programs. We will also test our students in groups with projects, it could be buiding a website, writing codes or designing a page. Each week quiz will be different in a catchy and breathtaking way.

Teach One Another

Charlie Girls coding is a club that will be there forever. This teach one another will be a place where by a novice can ask a pro a question or, a pro can teach a noivce when they a stuck with a work or do not understand something.
